Audi R8 Animation

Animation

Using Cinema 4D and a car build, we were able to customize and animate our vehicle to function just like a real car. With the addition of lighting and camera angles, we were able to showcase our car to the best of or abilities.

Production

From the Cinema 4D renders, I was able to create a full video incorporating both our renders as well as special effects. I used both Final Cut Pro as well as After Effects to create the masterpiece. One of the main challenges was communicating with the main animator on what kind of renders I wanted specifically for the video.

Web Design

For the car app, I was responsible for prototyping out the look and feel for the application. Featuring a dark colour scheme and high contrast image, I think I was successful at achieving the modern and sleek look we were going for throughout our entire assignment.

Process

In a group of three, we decided to divide the tasks then aid one another if need be. My first task was to create the application prototype. Once I was done the prototype, I began looking for music while my other group members began animating the car. Looking for music took a while as I wanted to ensure that it suited our video perfectly, nevertheless, upon finding the perfect audio, I aided my team in the animation. With the animation, I began compiling the initial draft of the commercial. With the input of my team, we produced the commercial we are proud of.
