CAISA Fashion Show 20th Anniversary

Last year, CFS celebrated its 20th anniversary. We wanted to share our success with everyone and let them know how much work really goes into each show and how many hours have been accumulated over the years.

In this short video, I put all of the numbers together in a creative way.

For this video, I was responsible for all of the content entirely from gathering footage to creating text. I am very prod at how it turned out as it received a lot of interaction through the CFS social media pages.
