Photography Cheat Sheet

Using a DSLR can be challenging especially for beginngers, therefore, I decided to make a cheat sheet to help those who are looking to begin using a DSLR.

I made this cheat sheet easy to understand as well as fun to read with bright and light colours. Photography is a fun hobby that should not be perceived as complicated due to the plethora of buttons found on a camera. With this infographic, I intend on making photography easier for everyone - beginners or professionals.
