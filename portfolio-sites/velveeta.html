Velveeta Wine Branding

Stay golden with liquid gold!

Process

1. Research

Prior to this assignment, I was completely unaware of what Velveeta was. With extensive research, I was able to get a better grasp at the branding of the product line. Once this research was done, I began to brainstorm.

2. Brainstorm

I wanted to take the childhood favourite product and make a brand new modern design out of it. This was challenging at first as product already had such an established branding style with its bold primary colours and child-like patterns. The existing branding did not suit the modern aesthetic that I wanted to accomplish. It took hours and many disposable ideas for me to finally hit my eureka moment.

3. Design

It finally dawned on me how I would achieve my modern branding of the cheese line. I took the underline of the logo and transformed that into a full mosaic that would make the base of my label. I then took the V from the logo and used that as the focus of my logo. After sketching and brainstorming, I decided to make the "V" itself a lighthouse style window in the label so that the wine would show through it. I thought this would make the label more interesting and eye-catching as that is not a commonly used design.

4. Style Guide

With so much work done in regards to the branding ideas of my product, I wanted to display them using a style guide. In my style guide, I included information on my style ideaas as well as advertisements that I had made also in the style of Velveeta.
