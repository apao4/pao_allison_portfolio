Spectrum Brand Presentation

I was asked by BMO Financial Group to create a presentation for their end of year conference to summarize one of their clients' sales with an engaging and friendly presentation. They asked for this specific presentation to be less formal to give the participants a break from the overall serious conference.

I stuck with the Spectrum branding and worked to incporate BMO branding as well. The clients loved the final product and were impressed with how well the information was translated even with the usage of colourful slides - something they were new to.
