CAISA Fashion Show Casual BTS

Each year, CAISA Fashion Show puts on the largest student run fashion show in Canada. Amongst the five scenes includng lingerie, semi formal, formal, sportswear, and casual, casual remains my favourite each year with its bold fashion and style. Not only is it my favourite scene in terms of the fashion that comes out of it but also to film and edit. With its loud colours and funky patterns, I find interest in it each year.

This specific year, the casual scene featured bright streetwear that emulated graffiti filled alleys. This photoshoot was shot at a local arcade featuring bright colours and neon lights.
